I currently work at the University of Lausanne, as FNS chercheuse senior (postdoc) in the project Gustave Roud "Œuvres complètes".
My research interests are digital humanities, textual criticism, medieval literature studies and the history of these disciplines.
My education is in romance philology (bachelor, master, PhD) and digital humanities (master). I've studied at Sapienza Università di Roma, Universidade Nova de Lisboa, École nationale des chartes. From 2014 to 2017 I've been part of the Marie Curie ITN DiXiT (Digital Scholarly Editions Initial Training), based at the Dutch Royal Academy of Arts and Science (Huygens Institute).
