I currently work at the University of Lausanne, as chercheuse FNS senior (PostDoc) for the digital edition of "Gustave Roud, Œuvres complètes". Within this project, I work on digital technologies applicable to Textual and Literary Criticism, from data analysis through modelling to publishing.

I've taught in specialized courses about Digital Philology and introductions to Digital Humanities in Switzerland, France, Italy, Netherlands, Greece.
As an active member of the DH community, I've been part from 2017 to 2020 of the Executive Committee of the European Association for Digital Humanities (EADH).

I've been previously a Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellow in the ITN DiXiT (Digital Scholarly Editions Initial Training), based at the Dutch Royal Academy of Arts and Sciences (Huygens Institute). My education is in Romance Philology (bachelor, master, PhD) and Digital Humanities (master) and I studied at Sapienza Università di Roma, Universidade Nova de Lisboa, École nationale des chartes.

⮬  Ongoing projects

  • Gustave Roud. Œuvres complètes. Pour en savoir plus, voici la page du projet.

  • Collate medieval languages. With Jean-Baptiste Camps et Lucence Ing, we try to develop solutions for the alignment and collation of medieval texts in multiple witnesses. Here is our working repo.

  • L'Archivio Monaci e eXist-DB. Nel post sul Blog di DiXiT, qualche informazione sull'edizione digitale dei documenti dell'Archivio Erneso Monaci e sull'utilizzo di eXist-DB e del TEI-Publisher (inclusa l'installazione di eXist su un server). Per il Laboratorio Monaci, vd. LabMonaci.uniroma1.it.

  • La Commedia di Boccaccio. La Commedia di Boccaccio is an ongoing project carried on with Sonia Tempestini, investigating the varia lectio of the three manuscripts of the Divina Commedia copied by Giovanni Boccaccio. Read about the project in a post at FontegaiaBlog or visit the website.

  • Studi sul Lancelot en prose. Je poursuis les recherches sur la tradition manuscrite du Lancelot en prose commencées pendant le doctorat. Tesi di dottorato di ricerca in Scienze del Testo, XXVII ciclo, Sapienza Università di Roma [IRIS Sapienza].
